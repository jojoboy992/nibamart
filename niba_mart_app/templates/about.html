Welcome to NiBa Mart, the first-of-its-kind online marketplace with a sleek and modern design built exclusively for students from Nile and Baze University!

We created this platform with one simple goal: to make buying, selling, and swapping goods between students easier, safer, and smarter.

Whether you're looking to sell your old textbooks, find affordable furniture for your dorm or even get your hands on limited-edition merch, NiBa Mart is your go-to space. No more long social media threads, unreliable meetups, or overpriced deals – just a clean, trusted, student-to-student shopping experience.

FAQ's

Can we be trusted?
All users are required to create an account using their Student Email Address(which will be verified) to ensure the legitimacy of all users on our platform, and each user is given a profile page where more informations about them are provided or given.
Why We Exist?
Students have stuffs, Lots of it. From laptops and lab coats to art supplies and kitchen gear – much of it in great condition, just waiting to be reused. But there was no reliable way for students across our two universities to connect and exchange items... until now.
Who It's For?
This marketplace is built by students, for students of Nile and Baze University. That means:

- Location-friendly transactions

- Affordable student-budget-friendly pricing

Whether you’re moving out, decluttering, or just looking for a deal, this space was built with you in mind.
Is This Site For Only Nile And Baze Students?

Yes, Currently our platform is exclusively available to only students of Nile and Baze University. We believe in fostering a close-knit community where students can easily connect and trade with their peers. However, we are always exploring opportunities to expand our services in the future. Stay tuned for any updates!

Our Mission
To connect students from Nile and Baze University through a trusted marketplace where they can:

- Buy and sell pre-loved items affordably

- Reduce waste and promote sustainable shopping

- Build a stronger campus community through peer-to-peer exchange
